C3LLFI3

Collaborative 3D Image Segmentation

Each cube is an image of a part of a plant stem cell in its natural 3D form. Your job is to segment each of these images correctly.

Instructions:

1.
Scroll up and down in the z direction to see cell walls.

2.
Move your mouse back and forth to see cell walls in the y direction.

3. Place "cell centers" where there are no cell walls.

4. Check the resulting 3D meshes. Edit or redo your segmentation if there are any non-convex cells, especially if they have an hourglass shape.
